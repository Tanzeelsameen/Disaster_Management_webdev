National Disaster Recovery Plan Resources

National Disaster Management Authority (NDMA)

The NDMA is responsible for planning, coordination, and implementation of disaster management activities across the country.

State Disaster Management Authorities (SDMAs)

Each state has its own SDMA responsible for managing disasters at the state level.

National Disaster Response Force (NDRF)

The NDRF is a specialized force trained in disaster response and rescue operations.

Central and State Government Agencies

Various government agencies play critical roles in disaster management.

Early Warning Systems

Early warning systems are implemented to alert people about impending disasters.

Disaster Management Plans

The NDMA and SDMAs develop comprehensive disaster management plans.
