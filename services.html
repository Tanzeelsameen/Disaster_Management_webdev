NDRPI Services

Emergency Response

We provide immediate assistance during disasters, including search and rescue operations, medical support, and evacuation procedures.

Emergency Shelter and Basic Needs

Establishing and managing emergency shelters for displaced individuals and families. Distributing essential supplies such as food, water, clothing, and personal hygiene items. Providing temporary housing solutions, including tents or prefabricated structures.

Medical Assistance and Healthcare

Setting up temporary medical clinics to provide immediate healthcare services. Ensuring access to necessary medications, vaccinations, and medical supplies. Coordinating medical personnel and volunteers to provide medical assistance and mental health support.

Search and Rescue Operations

Conducting search and rescue operations to locate and rescue trapped or missing individuals. Deploying trained personnel, including emergency responders, firefighters, and specialized rescue teams. Collaborating with local authorities and organizations to coordinate search and rescue efforts.

Disaster Preparedness

We work to enhance public awareness and preparedness through educational campaigns, training programs, and community drills.

Education and School Support

Organizing educational programs to support children's emotional well-being and academic progress.

Risk Assessment and Planning

Our team conducts comprehensive risk assessments to identify vulnerable areas and develop strategies to mitigate potential disasters.

Coordination and Communication

We facilitate effective coordination and communication among various agencies, stakeholders, and communities during emergencies.

Disaster Recovery and Rehabilitation

We assist in the recovery and rehabilitation process after a disaster, including the provision of temporary shelters, medical support, and infrastructure rebuilding.
